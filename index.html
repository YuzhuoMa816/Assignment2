The World Happiness Report Visualisation

This report use the data from 149 country to show us the factors that affect happiness score

Happiness top chart

The yello line in the bar chart is the average happiness score regards the 149 countries, the right small version showed the whole sorted countries distribution.

Happiness_map

The user can select the zoom center to different region and zoom in to clearly see the detail countries' happiness score distrubution.





Happiness heat map

Select region at botton to focus on the region.The Heat Map demonstrate how the factor(Freedom_to_Make_Lifechoice) and factor(Social-Support) related to the happiness score. Color represent the happiness score

Happiness bubble chart

select region at botton to focus on the region click the right region sign to hightlight region. The bubble chart here demonstrate how the healthy life expectancy and logged GDP Per Capita related to the happiness score x-axis represents healthy life expectancy, y-axis represents logged GDP Per Capita bbubble size represent happiness score



Happies pie chart

The Radial Plot showed the countries distribution along the world from 149 countries Put the mouse on and click to focus
